## Template 4: Phone Script (for companies with only phone numbers)

**Opening:**
"Hej, jag ringer från Industrin.net. Vi såg att [COMPANY NAME] är listad hos oss, och vi undrar om ni vill uppdatera er profil?"

**Key points to mention:**
1. Their profile already exists: [PROFILE URL]
2. It's free and takes 2 minutes
3. They can claim it here: [CLAIM URL]
4. They'll be able to receive quote requests from customers
5. Better visibility in search results

**If they're interested:**
"Perfekt! Jag skickar er en länk via SMS/email så kan ni ansöka när det passar. Vad är bästa e-postadressen att skicka till?"

**If they're not interested:**
"Okej, inga problem. Om ni ändrar er kan ni alltid ansöka via [CLAIM URL]. Ha en bra dag!"

## Tips for Outreach

1. **Personalize**: Mention their specific company name and what's missing
2. **Keep it short**: Busy business owners appreciate brevity
3. **Highlight value**: Focus on "receive customer requests" - that's the main benefit
4. **Make it easy**: Include the direct claim URL
5. **Follow up**: Send a reminder after 1-2 weeks if no response

## Metrics to Track

- Number of emails sent
- Open rate (if using email marketing tool)
- Click-through rate on claim URLs
- Actual claims submitted
- Response rate
